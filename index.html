  • League Rules
    • All players must register here before joining league games.
    • Games can be either live or async, but must include 4 players
    • All games must be played with Advanced Setup (AdSet).
    • Maps must be randomly selected, but hosts are free to choose their preferred deck (standard or E&P) and clearing distribution (fixed or random).
    • Games are forfeit (DQ) if any player is replaced by AI.
    • Two-way coalitions are allowed, three-way coalitions are banned.
    • Player league scores are recorded as 1=win, .5=coalition win, 0=loss.
    • All game business including table talk must take place within the game thread in the #root-lfg or inapp chat (no DMs).
    • Only players that log at least 15 games over the season qualify for leaderboard and award consideration.
